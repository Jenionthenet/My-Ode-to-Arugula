Designer Salad

My first known encounter with arugula was when I had it in a pasta dish in a restaruant in New York City. I guess this experience stuck out in my mind because a lettuce was being put in a pasta dish, and my only previous experience with that was with spinach.

I enjoy eating arugula the following ways:

  • in salads
  • in pasta dishes
  • on top of brick oven pizzas

The name of this page is "Designer Salad" because my mother always teases me and calls arugula designer salad whenever I use it in meals. She is a romain salad eater and says that arugula is not sturdy enough. I understand where she is coming from, but I stil love me some arugula.
